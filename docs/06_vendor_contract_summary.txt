EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

Nexus Technology Solutions maintains 64 active vendor relationships. Total
annual vendor spend for FY2025 was $4.1 million, comprising approximately
$2.5 million in software and cloud services and $1.6 million in hardware,
support contracts, and professional services.

The 10 largest vendors by annual spend represent 71% of total vendor spend.
Eleven contracts are scheduled for renewal in the next 6 months. Three vendor
relationships are under strategic review.

Strategic Review Notes:
Broadcom's acquisition of VMware and subsequent licensing model changes have
significantly increased Nexus' per-host costs. At contract renewal Broadcom
is requiring migration to the VMware Cloud Foundation (VCF) subscription model.
Estimated renewal cost under VCF: $380,000-420,000/year (52-69% increase).

Alternatives under active evaluation:
1. Nutanix AHV — Marcus Chen leading evaluation, POC in DC-CHI dev cluster
2. Microsoft Hyper-V + SCVMM — Less feature-rich but zero additional license
   cost under existing EA
3. Proxmox VE — Open source, significant migration risk, under review
4. Maintain VMware, negotiate VCF pricing — may achieve 20-25% below list

Decision Required: By June 2026 to allow migration time before September
contract expiration. Budget impact: $80,000-$172,000 annual increase depending
on decision. This is a significant financial decision requiring Board awareness.
